# UniPilot — Pricing and Payment (التسعير وطرق الدفع)

---

## Pricing

- **Base price:** $10,000 USD (or equivalent) for a **one-time perpetual license**.
- **Includes:**
  - Full source code (frontend + backend).
  - Documentation (Runbook, Admin Guide, Architecture, Backup/Restore, Security, API Overview, Product Spec).
  - Support period (e.g. 30 or 90 days; see SUPPORT_POLICY.md).
  - Handover (e.g. deployment walkthrough and/or handover video).

- **Alternatives (to be agreed with buyer):**
  - **Installment:** e.g. 50% on signing, 50% on handover.
  - **Annual license:** If preferred instead of perpetual, price and terms to be defined in the contract.
  - **Custom features or SLA:** Priced separately.

---

## Payment Methods

- **Bank transfer:** Payment to seller’s bank account (details on invoice).
- **Other methods:** Stripe, PayPal, or other methods can be agreed and documented in the contract or invoice.

---

## Invoice

- A formal invoice will be issued using **INVOICE_TEMPLATE.md** (or exported to PDF) with:
  - Seller and buyer details.
  - Description: “UniPilot — Perpetual license and support package.”
  - Amount: $10,000 (or agreed amount).
  - Tax if applicable (VAT, etc.) according to seller’s jurisdiction.
  - Payment terms and due date.

---

## Contract

- Sale and license terms are defined in **LICENSE_AGREEMENT_TEMPLATE.md** (or CONTRACT_TEMPLATE). Signing the agreement and paying the invoice completes the purchase.
