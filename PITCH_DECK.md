# UniPilot — Pitch Deck (Content for 10–15 Slides)

Use this content to build a short pitch deck (e.g. PowerPoint, Google Slides, or HTML).

1. **Title.** UniPilot — Your Smart Academic Companion. Subtitle: For universities and institutions. One slide.

2. **Problem.** Students and faculty struggle with fragmented tools: grades here, schedules there, no single place for AI-assisted study and planning. Institutions need a unified, secure, Arabic-ready platform.

3. **Solution.** UniPilot: one dashboard for courses, grades, planner, AI coach, study tools, voice, and admin control. Built for students and scalable for institutions.

4. **Features (1).** Dashboard, courses, grades, academic history, planner (events + tasks), AI-generated plans and suggestions.

5. **Features (2).** AI Coach (chat with context), study tools (summaries, flashcards, quiz, mind map), voice-to-text, TTS, theses/research help, diagrams/infographics.

6. **Admin & scale.** Admin panel: users, catalog, broadcast notifications. Supports thousands of students and hundreds of admins. PostgreSQL, secure auth, rate limiting.

7. **Languages & UX.** Arabic and English. Light/dark theme. RTL. Responsive.

8. **Security & privacy.** JWT, bcrypt, Helmet, rate limiting. Export and delete data. Terms and Privacy Policy. See SECURITY.md.

9. **Tech stack.** React, Vite, Express, PostgreSQL. Deploy on Render (or Docker/VPS). One codebase, one deployment unit.
