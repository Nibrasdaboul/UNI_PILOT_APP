# UniPilot — Support Policy (سياسة الدعم)

---

## Support period

- **Included:** [30 / 90] days of technical support from the date of delivery (code + documentation + handover).
- **Scope:** Deployment issues, clarification of documentation, and **defect fixing** for the delivered UniPilot code. Support does not include new feature development unless agreed separately.

---

## Contact channel

- **Email:** [your-support@email.com]  
  or  
- **Dedicated channel:** [e.g. Slack/Discord/Teams link or ticket system]

- **Response:** We aim to respond within [1–2] business days. Critical issues (e.g. production down) will be prioritized.

---

## What we will help with

- Deploying the app (Render, Docker, or VPS).
- Configuring environment variables and database.
- Understanding RUNBOOK, ADMIN_GUIDE, ARCHITECTURE, BACKUP_RESTORE, and API_OVERVIEW.
- Fixing bugs in the delivered code (reproducible with the provided docs and version).

---

## What is out of scope

- Custom feature development (new modules, integrations, or UI changes beyond the delivered product).
- Third-party services (e.g. GROQ, Render) beyond basic configuration as documented.
- On-site or phone support unless agreed in writing.
- Support after the support period (can be extended under a separate agreement).

---

## Security and updates

- Security-related issues in the delivered code will be addressed during the support period. For how long we provide security updates after that, see **SECURITY_UPDATES_POLICY.md**.
