# UniPilot — Promo Video Script (2–3 min)

Use this script to record a short promotional video (e.g. for a website or pitch).

---

## Scene 1 — Hook (0:00–0:20)

- **Visual:** Student at laptop or on phone, looking at a messy calendar and multiple apps.
- **Voiceover:** “Grades in one app, schedule in another, and no single place to plan your semester. What if one platform did it all?”

---

## Scene 2 — UniPilot intro (0:20–0:45)

- **Visual:** UniPilot logo and dashboard. Show clean layout: courses, GPA, upcoming tasks.
- **Voiceover:** “UniPilot is your smart academic companion. One dashboard for your courses, grades, and tasks — in Arabic and English.”

---

## Scene 3 — Key features (0:45–1:45)

- **Planner:** Add event, add task, daily view. “Plan your week and never miss a deadline.”
- **AI Coach:** Open chat, type a question, show reply. “Ask the AI coach anything — study tips, explanations, or next steps.”
- **Study tools:** Quick shot of summaries, flashcards, or quiz. “Summarize, practice, and track your progress.”
- **Grades:** Add a grade, show final. “Track your GPA and stay on top of your performance.”

---

## Scene 4 — For institutions (1:45–2:15)

- **Visual:** Admin panel (users, catalog, or notifications). “For universities: manage users, courses, and send announcements — all in one place.”
- **Voiceover:** “Secure, scalable, and ready for your institution.”

---

## Scene 5 — CTA (2:15–2:35)

- **Visual:** UniPilot logo, tagline, and contact or link.
- **Voiceover:** “UniPilot. One platform. Your success. Contact us for a demo or license.”

---

## Checklist

- [ ] Record in 1080p; ensure Arabic/English UI is readable.
- [ ] Use light theme or high contrast for clarity.
- [ ] Keep background music low so voiceover is clear.
- [ ] Add captions (AR/EN) if possible.
